What is this?

Hey there, I'm Cory Parsnipson and {{funfact}}!

I made this app to let people disassemble Intel 8080 CPU binaries using an online service.

What's an Intel 8080 processor? In a nutshell, it was a popular microprocessor designed in the 70's and is very similar to the CPU used in the original gameboy.

Also, you're probably wondering what a disassembler is. A disassembler is kind of like the opposite of a compiler. Instead of compiling human readable programs into binary, it takes binaries and "decompiles" them into human readable "assembly language".

If you want to see how this app displays disassemblies, you can click on examples in the sub-navigation.

Or if you'd rather upload a binary (aka ROM) and watch it in action, here are some samples you can download to try out the app:

  • Part 1 of an 8080 Space Invaders game
  • An 8080 benchmark called CPUTEST.COM
